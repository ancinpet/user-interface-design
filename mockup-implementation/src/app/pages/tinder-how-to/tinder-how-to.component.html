How to use Tinder search
Guide

Tinder Search is a simple function that lets you quickly find what you are looking for using your previous decisions. To start, set your preferences and click on the search button. After, you will be shown one car and decide whether you like it, dislike it, or want to save it into your favorites. As you look at more cars, our application learns and will provide you with better results.
